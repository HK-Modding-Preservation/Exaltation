# Charm List

This file contains a list of all charm glorifications, their sprites, and their effects. Vanilla effects are not listed, but each glorified charm preserves its old effects.

## Gathering Swarm -> Symbol of Avarice

![Gathering Swarm](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/1.png?raw=true)

Rather than picking up Geo after a few moments, any Geo will be picked up instantly as soon as it spawns.

Glorified by defeating Great Nailsage Sly.

## Wayward Compass -> Lifeseed Lantern

![Wayward Compass](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/2.png?raw=true)

Costs zero notches, and adds one lifeblood mask.

Glorified by defeating Brothers Oro and Mato.

## Grubsong -> Glubberfly Hymn

![Grubsong](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/3.png?raw=true)

Increases the amount of SOUL gained on nail hit by 1 per two missing masks of health.

Glorified by completing the Pantheon of the Master with SOUL bound, or defeating the ascended Collector.

## Stalwart Shell -> Kingsmould Carapace

![Shell](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/4.png?raw=true)

When taking damage above one masks, SOUL may be automatically consumed to prevent one mask of that damage.

The amount of soul starts at one-third of your maximum SOUL meter, and doubles with each hit. This doubled amount caps at a full SOUL meter, and decreases by 3% SOUL every second.

Glorified by completing the Pantheon of the Master with SHELL bound, or defeating the ascended Failed Champion.

## Baldur Shell -> Baldur Stoneshell

![Shell2](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/5.png?raw=true)

The shell regains one hit every 10 seconds that no damage is taken.

Glorified by defeating Brothers Oro and Mato.

## Fury of the Fallen -> Wyrmfury / Shadefury

![FotF1](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/6.png?raw=true) ![FotF2](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/6_shade.png?raw=true)

Has a different appearance depending on if you have the Void Heart or not.
Nullifies a single hit that would kill you, and increases nail damage while at one mask by an additional 15%.

Glorified by defeating Brothers Oro and Mato.

## Quick Focus -> Swift Focus

![Focus](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/7.png?raw=true)

Greatly increases the speed of focusing SOUL.

Glorified by completing the Pantheon of the Knight with SOUL bound, or defeating the ascended Pure Vessel.

## Lifeblood Heart -> Lifeblood Crux

![LB1](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/8.png?raw=true)

Increases lifeblood masks by four, instead of two.

Glorified by defeating Paintmaster Sheo.

## Lifeblood Core -> Lifeblood Nucleus

![LB2](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/9.png?raw=true)

Increases lifeblood masks by six, instead of four.

Glorified by defeating Paintmaster Sheo.

## Thorns of Agony -> Palace Rose

![Thorns](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/12.png?raw=true)

Deals 2.5x nail damage instead of 2x, and deals spell damage instead of generic damage.

Glorified by defeating Paintmaster Sheo.

## Steady Body -> Garden Sprig

![Sprig](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/14.png?raw=true)

Costs no notches.

Glorified by defeating Brothers Oro and Mato.

## Sharp Shadow -> Razor Shadow

![SS](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/16.png?raw=true)

Dash damage is increased to 2x nail damage. Gain 0.6 seconds of extended invulnerability after the shade dash finishes.

Glorified by completing the Pantheon of the Sage with NAIL bound or defeating ascended Nosk.

## Shaman Stone -> Shaman Relic

![Egg](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/19.png?raw=true)

Increases spell damage by an additional 12.5%.

Glorified by completing the Pantheon of the Sage with SHELL bound or defeating ascended Soul Tyrant.

## Soul Catcher -> Soul Snare

![Catcher](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/20.png?raw=true)

Captures 5% additional SOUL on nail hit, instead of 3%.

Glorified by defeating Great Nailsage Sly.

## Soul Eater -> Soul Feeder

![Eater](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/21.png?raw=true)

Captures 11% additional SOUL on nail hit, instead of 8%.

Glorified by completing the Pantheon of the Sage with SOUL bound or defeating ascended Great Nailsage Sly.

## Glowing Womb -> Primal Womb

![Womb](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/22.png?raw=true)

Spawns hatchlings 50% faster, and has 8 maximum hatchlings, up from 4. Costs 4 SOUL per hatchling instead of 8.

Glorified by defeating Paintmaster Sheo.

## Nailmaster's Glory -> Nailsage's Tenacity / Nailsage's Patience

![NMG](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/26.png?raw=true) ![NMGP](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/26_patience.png?raw=true)

Nailsage's Tenacity increases nail damage by 3% per mask of missing health.

Nailsage's Patience causes nail hits to bypass armor but gain no SOUL.

Glorified by defeating Great Nailsage Sly. Defeat him again to swap between the two.

## Joni's Blessing -> Joni's Benediction

![Joni](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/27.png?raw=true)

Provides four additional lifeblood masks.

Glorified by completing the Pantheon of the Sage with SHELL bound, or defeating radiant Great Nailsage Sly.

## Hiveblood -> Ambrosial Ampoule

![HB](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/29.png?raw=true)

Costs three notches instead of four, and regenerates health 20% faster.

Glorified by defeating the Pure Vessel.

## Dashmaster -> Stagway Coin

![DM](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/31.png?raw=true)

Costs one notch instead of two.

Glorified by defeating Paintmaster Sheo.

## Quick Slash -> Steel Tempest

![Buzzsaw](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/32.png?raw=true)

Uncaps nail swing speed.

Glorified by completing the Pantheon of the Knight with CHARMS bound or defeating radiant Brothers Oro and Mato.

## Spell Twister -> Prismatic Lens

![Lens](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/33.png?raw=true)

Increases spell damage depending on remaining SOUL after cast. Every 4 SOUL increases damage by 1%, to a max of +25%.

Glorified by completing the Pantheon of the Artist with SOUL bound or defeating ascended Uumuu.

## Sprintmaster -> Marathon Master

![Coin](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/37.png?raw=true)

Increases movement speed by 40%, instead of 20%. Combine with Stagway Coin for shenanigans.

Glorified by defeating Paintmaster Sheo.

## Void Heart <-> Lordsoul

![Lordsoul](https://github.com/TheodoreChristianRadu/Exaltation/blob/master/Resources/Charms/36.png?raw=true)

Same effect as Kingsoul for three notches instead of five.

Glorified by defeating the radiant Pure Vessel. Defeat Pure Vessel to swap between the two.

## Grimmchild <-> Carefree Melody

Defeat Nightmare King Grimm in the Hall of Gods to swap between the two.
